## Part F: Word Problems

27. The temperature in the morning was -5°C. By noon it rose to 3°C. What type of numbers are these?

28. You have $\frac{3}{4}$ of a pizza. Your friend has 0.5 of a pizza. Who has more pizza?

## Solutions

### Part A Solutions

1. **7**: Natural, Whole, Integer, Rational, Real
2. **-3**: Integer, Rational, Real
3. **0**: Whole, Integer, Rational, Real
4. **$\frac{2}{5}$**: Fraction, Rational, Real
5. **0.75**: Decimal, Rational, Real
6. **-15**: Integer, Rational, Real
7. **$\sqrt{2}$**: Irrational, Real (cannot be written as a fraction)
8. **$\frac{10}{2}$ = 5**: Natural, Whole, Integer, Rational, Real

### Part B Solutions

9. **Order:** -5, -2, 0, 1, 3, 4
```
  -5   -2    0    1    3    4
   •----•----•----•----•----•-->
```

10. **Order:** -3.5, -1, 0, 1.5, 2.5
```
  -3.5  -1    0   1.5  2.5
    •----•----•----•----•-->
```

### Part C Solutions

11. $|8| = 8$
12. $|-12| = 12$
13. $|0| = 0$
14. $|-7| + |3| = 7 + 3 = 10$
15. $|5 - 9| = |-4| = 4$

### Part D Solutions

16. 5 **<** 8 (5 is less than 8)
17. -3 **<** -1 (negative numbers: closer to zero is larger)
18. 0 **>** -4 (zero is greater than any negative)
19. $\frac{1}{2}$ **=** 0.5 (they're equal!)
20. -10 **>** -15 (negative numbers: -10 is closer to zero)

### Part E Solutions

**Fractions to decimals:**
21. $\frac{1}{4} = 0.25$
22. $\frac{3}{5} = 0.6$
23. $\frac{7}{8} = 0.875$

**Decimals to fractions:**
24. $0.6 = \frac{6}{10} = \frac{3}{5}$
25. $0.25 = \frac{25}{100} = \frac{1}{4}$
26. $0.125 = \frac{125}{1000} = \frac{1}{8}$

### Part F Solutions

27. **Integers** (both -5 and 3 are integers)

28. **You have more!** $\frac{3}{4} = 0.75 > 0.5$

29. **Negative integer** (-120, below zero/sea level)

30. **From worst to best:** -$30, -$15, $0, $25, $50
    (Most debt to most money)
