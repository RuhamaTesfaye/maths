## Solutions

### Part A Solutions
1. $24 \div 6 = 4$ (Check: $4 \times 6 = 24$ ✓)
2. $56 \div 7 = 8$ (Check: $8 \times 7 = 56$ ✓)
3. $81 \div 9 = 9$ (Check: $9 \times 9 = 81$ ✓)
4. $48 \div 8 = 6$ (Check: $6 \times 8 = 48$ ✓)
5. $63 \div 7 = 9$ (Check: $9 \times 7 = 63$ ✓)

### Part B Solutions
6. $(-30) \div 5 = -6$ (different signs → negative)
7. $(-48) \div (-6) = 8$ (same signs → positive)
8. $36 \div (-9) = -4$ (different signs → negative)
9. $(-72) \div (-8) = 9$ (same signs → positive)
10. $(-100) \div 10 = -10$ (different signs → negative)

### Part C Solutions
11. $156 \div 12 = 13$ (Check: $13 \times 12 = 156$ ✓)
12. $273 \div 13 = 21$ (Check: $21 \times 13 = 273$ ✓)
13. $384 \div 16 = 24$ (Check: $24 \times 16 = 384$ ✓)
14. $525 \div 25 = 21$ (Check: $21 \times 25 = 525$ ✓)

### Part D Solutions
15. $17 \div 5 = 3$ **remainder 2** (or 3.4)
16. $43 \div 6 = 7$ **remainder 1** (or 7.166...)
17. $100 \div 7 = 14$ **remainder 2** (or 14.285...)

18. $17 \div 5 = 3\frac{2}{5}$
19. $43 \div 6 = 7\frac{1}{6}$

### Part E Solutions
20. $\frac{3}{4} \div \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{2}{1} = \frac{6}{4} = \frac{3}{2} = 1\frac{1}{2}$

21. $\frac{2}{3} \div \frac{4}{5} = \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{5}{4} = \frac{10}{12} = \frac{5}{6}$

22. $\frac{5}{6} \div \frac{2}{3} = \frac{5}{6} \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{15}{12} = \frac{5}{4} = 1\frac{1}{4}$

23. $\frac{7}{8} \div \frac{1}{4} = \frac{7}{8} \times \frac{4}{1} = \frac{28}{8} = \frac{7}{2} = 3\frac{1}{2}$

24. $\frac{3}{5} \div 2 = \frac{3}{5} \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{10}$

### Part F Solutions
25. $2\frac{1}{2} \div 1\frac{1}{4} = \frac{5}{2} \div \frac{5}{4} = \frac{5}{2} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{20}{10} = 2$

26. $3\frac{1}{3} \div \frac{2}{3} = \frac{10}{3} \div \frac{2}{3} = \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{30}{6} = 5$

27. $1\frac{1}{2} \div 3 = \frac{3}{2} \div 3 = \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}$

### Part G Solutions
28. $12.6 \div 3 = 4.2$
29. $8.4 \div 2.1 = 4$ (move decimals: $84 \div 21 = 4$)
30. $7.5 \div 0.5 = 15$ (move decimals: $75 \div 5 = 15$)
31. $4.8 \div 0.6 = 8$ (move decimals: $48 \div 6 = 8$)
32. $9 \div 0.3 = 30$ (move decimals: $90 \div 3 = 30$)

### Part H Solutions
33. $450 \div 10 = 45$ (move decimal 1 place left)
34. $3400 \div 100 = 34$ (move decimal 2 places left)
35. $25000 \div 1000 = 25$ (move decimal 3 places left)
36. $230 \div 5 = (230 \times 2) \div 10 = 460 \div 10 = 46$

### Part I Solutions
37. $0 \div 7 = 0$ (zero divided by anything is zero)
38. $15 \div 1 = 15$ (dividing by 1 doesn't change the number)
39. $9 \div 9 = 1$ (any number divided by itself is 1)
40. **UNDEFINED!** You cannot divide by zero. It's mathematically impossible.

### Part J Solutions
41. $(-\frac{3}{4}) \div (-\frac{1}{2}) = (-\frac{3}{4}) \times (-\frac{2}{1}) = \frac{6}{4} = \frac{3}{2} = 1\frac{1}{2}$

42. $(-12.5) \div 2.5 = -5$ (different signs → negative)

43. $\frac{2\frac{1}{3}}{1\frac{1}{6}} = \frac{7}{3} \div \frac{7}{6} = \frac{7}{3} \times \frac{6}{7} = \frac{42}{21} = 2$

44. $(48 \div 6) \div 2 = 8 \div 2 = 4$

### Part K Solutions
45. $24 \div 6 = 4$ cookies per friend

46. $15.6 \div 1.3 = 12$ pieces

47. $4\frac{1}{2} \div \frac{3}{4} = \frac{9}{2} \times \frac{4}{3} = \frac{36}{6} = 6$ people

48. $240 \div 12 = 20$ miles per gallon
