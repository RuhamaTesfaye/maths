## Solutions

### Part A Solutions
1. $7 \times 8 = 56$
2. $9 \times 6 = 54$
3. $12 \times 5 = 60$
4. $15 \times 4 = 60$
5. $11 \times 11 = 121$

### Part B Solutions
6. $(-5) \times 6 = -30$ (different signs → negative)
7. $(-8) \times (-7) = 56$ (same signs → positive)
8. $9 \times (-4) = -36$ (different signs → negative)
9. $(-10) \times (-10) = 100$ (same signs → positive)
10. $(-3) \times 12 = -36$ (different signs → negative)

### Part C Solutions
11. $\frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{8}{15}$

12. $\frac{1}{2} \times \frac{3}{4} = \frac{3}{8}$

13. $\frac{5}{6} \times \frac{3}{10} = \frac{15}{60} = \frac{1}{4}$

14. $\frac{7}{8} \times \frac{4}{7} = \frac{28}{56} = \frac{1}{2}$ (or cross-cancel: $\frac{7}{8} \times \frac{4}{7} = \frac{1}{2}$)

15. $2 \times \frac{3}{5} = \frac{2}{1} \times \frac{3}{5} = \frac{6}{5} = 1\frac{1}{5}$

### Part D Solutions
16. $1\frac{1}{2} \times 2 = \frac{3}{2} \times 2 = \frac{6}{2} = 3$

17. $2\frac{1}{3} \times 1\frac{1}{2} = \frac{7}{3} \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{21}{6} = \frac{7}{2} = 3\frac{1}{2}$

18. $3\frac{1}{4} \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{13}{4} \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{26}{12} = \frac{13}{6} = 2\frac{1}{6}$

### Part E Solutions
19. $2.5 \times 4 = 10.0 = 10$
20. $3.2 \times 1.5 = 4.8$
21. $0.6 \times 0.8 = 0.48$
22. $1.25 \times 2.4 = 3.00 = 3.0$
23. $0.05 \times 0.3 = 0.015$

### Part F Solutions
24. $47 \times 10 = 470$ (move decimal 1 place right)
25. $3.5 \times 100 = 350$ (move decimal 2 places right)
26. $0.25 \times 1000 = 250$ (move decimal 3 places right)
27. $86 \times 5 = (86 \times 10) \div 2 = 860 \div 2 = 430$
28. $32 \times 4 = 32 \times 2 \times 2 = 64 \times 2 = 128$

### Part G Solutions
29. $5 \times 7 = 7 \times$ **5**
30. $(2 \times 3) \times 4 = 2 \times$ (**3** $\times$ 4)
31. $8 \times 1 =$ **8**
32. $9 \times 0 =$ **0**
33. $4 \times (5 + 3) = (4 \times 5) +$ (**4** $\times$ 3)

### Part H Solutions
34. $(-3) \times (-4) \times (-2) = 12 \times (-2) = -24$
35. $\frac{2}{5} \times \frac{5}{2} \times 10 = 1 \times 10 = 10$
36. $(2.5 \times 4) \times 0.1 = 10 \times 0.1 = 1$
37. $(-0.5) \times (-8) = 4$ (same signs → positive)

### Part I Solutions
